Pepperoni Pizza

Description

This is a recipe for a Pepperoni Pizza

Ingredients

For the dough

  • 1 teaspoon yeast
  • 2/3 cup warm water
  • flour
  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 1/2 tablespoon sugar
  • 1/2 tablespoon garlic powder
  • cornmeal to dust the pan

Sauce and toppings

  • 50ml passata
  • 1/2 tsp dried basil
  • 1 garlic clove, crushed
  • 80g mozzarella, grated
  • 8 pepperoni slices

Recipe

  1. Mix the dough ingredients.
  2. Knead the dough.
  3. Leave in a large bowl and cover, leave to rise for at least 90 minutes in the fridge.
  4. Punch down the dough to release air bubbles.
  5. Roll dough out into a 12 inch Pizza shape.
  6. Mix the passata, basil and garlic to make the pizza sauce.
  7. Smother the dough with the pizza sauce.
  8. Add the cheese and pepperoni.
  9. Bake in the oven for 15 minutes.
  10. Enjoy!
